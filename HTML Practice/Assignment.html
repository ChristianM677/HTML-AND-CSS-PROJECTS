Best Dog breed: Pomeranian

This is an opinion based list.

1. Pomeranians are cute

They are cute, perfect for kids because they are not scary like other breeds.

Pomeranians are very pretty, with their upright pointy ears and foxy faces, but it’s temperament that puts them center stage wherever they go. Supreme extroverts, the standard says they should exhibit intelligence and a vivacious spirit.
